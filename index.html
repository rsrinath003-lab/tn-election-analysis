Counterintuitive Finding:

DMK, a Periyarist party rooted in social justice ideology, consistently underperforms in SC-reserved seats. AIADMK holds a structural advantage here — likely due to welfare scheme loyalty and local caste alliance politics that cut across ideological lines.

2026 Election Outlook

Based purely on historical patterns from 5 elections. DMK is now the incumbent — and history is not kind to incumbents in Tamil Nadu.

The Baseline Prediction:

Every time DMK has been the outgoing government, it has averaged 27 seats — a collapse to ~11.5% of the assembly. The anti-incumbency pattern broke only once in 5 elections (2016, Jayalalithaa). Without an equivalent leader, 2026 should favour the opposition.
